About Us

Discover our journey and the people behind SweetBites Bakery

Welcome to SweetBites Bakery, your number one source for all things baked. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of baked goods, with an emphasis on quality, taste, and customer service.

Founded in 2023 by Hitesh Kandare, SweetBites Bakery has come a long way from its beginnings in a small kitchen. When Hitesh first started out, her passion for baking drove her to start her own business, and we are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
Hitesh Kandare, Founder
